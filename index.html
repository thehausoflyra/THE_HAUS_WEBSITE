BRAND ORIGIN

Why Haus of Lyra?

Every story deserves a guiding light.

The name Haus of Lyra is inspired by the Lyra constellation, the celestial harp. Lyra (the Harp) is a small but prominent northern hemisphere constellation famous for containing Vega, the fifth-brightest star in the night sky. In Greek mythology, Lyra represents the lyre of Orpheus, the legendary musician and poet. According to the myth, the god Apollo gave Orpheus the harp, which played music so beautiful that it could tame wild beasts and soothe any sorrow.

Just as the music of the harp was created to soothe, capture, and move people, I aim to create imagery that speaks to the soul and carries the moments that shape your future.

To represent this focus, I have taken the five brightest stars of the Lyra constellation and given each of them a meaning as my core brand pillars.
