Privacy Policy

Your trust is essential to my work. This policy outlines how Haus of Lyra collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information I Collect

When you inquire about photography or filmmaking services, use the contact forms, or enter into a contract with Haus of Lyra, I collect personal information that you voluntarily provide. This includes:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address and Phone Number
  • Event details (wedding date, locations, timeline details)
  • Billing and payment details necessary to process contracts

2. How I Use Your Information

I use your personal information solely to provide professional creative services. This includes:

  • Responding to your initial inquiries and scheduling consultations
  • Preparing contracts and managing booking details
  • Delivering online image galleries and cinematic video files
  • Processing payments and sending receipts

I will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Image Storage and Client Galleries

Your delivered photographs and films are hosted securely through trusted third-party gallery platforms (such as Pic-Time, Pixieset, or Vimeo). These platforms maintain their own security measures to safeguard access to your online galleries. By accessing these galleries, you agree to their respective terms.

4. Media Release & Portfolio Usage

As a creative business, I showcase completed photographs and films as part of my professional portfolio, website, and social media channels. Portfolio usage is always based on the consents outlined in our contract. If you wish to request the removal of a specific image or clip from my digital channels, you can contact me directly.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website uses cookies to enhance browsing experiences and analyze site traffic (for example, through basic visitor metrics). You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though some features of the site might function differently.

6. Changes to this Policy

I reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon their posting on this page.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy or wish to request modifications to your stored data, please email me at thehausoflyra@gmail.com.
