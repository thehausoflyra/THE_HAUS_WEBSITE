The Experience

This isn't a cookie-cutter session.

Before we ever pick up a camera, we talk. About you — your personality, your style, where you feel most like yourself. We work together to choose locations that make sense for who you are, not just wherever happens to be trendy right now.

On session day, I'm not handing you a list of poses to run through. I'm watching for the moments between poses — the laugh you can't hold back, the way you carry yourself when you forget the camera is there, the expression that actually looks like you. That's where the best photos live.

Your gallery is delivered within two to three weeks through a private online gallery. High resolution. No watermarks. Ready to download, share, and print.

Built Different
