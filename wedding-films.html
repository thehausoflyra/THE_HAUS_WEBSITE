Timeless Quality

Built around character, not trends.

At Haus of Lyra, I don't believe in templates or trendy filters that will feel dated in five years. I focus on creating cinematic films that feel timeless, emotional, and deeply personal.

Every couple is different, which means every film I cut is entirely unique. I don't force your day into a cookie-cutter structure; I craft a narrative that reflects who you actually are.

The result is a wedding film that feels less like a highlight recap and more like a time machine.

The Approach

Real audio. Natural interactions. Genuine emotion.

I document your day as it naturally unfolds, using beautiful cinematic visuals to elevate the underlying story. The goal isn't simply to show what happened on a Saturday in Iowa.

The goal is to make you feel it all over again.
