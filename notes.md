## Image Asset Uploads
When adding new galleries:
1. Upload full-resolution media to the Google Drive folder.
2. Upload web-optimized versions to your Hostinger server in the corresponding folder:
   - `/wedding-pictures/` for wedding photography/films
   - `/senior-pictures/` for senior portraits
   - `/vendor-images/` for vendor cards
